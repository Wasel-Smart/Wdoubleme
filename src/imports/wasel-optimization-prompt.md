Input: Current Wasel architecture, features, business model, and market context.

Consolidate Brands

Recommend a single unified platform structure:

Wasel
├── Ride
└── Send Package

Strengthen Unit Economics & Metrics

Review current revenue, pricing, and commission flows.

Add platform metrics layer including:

CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)

LTV (Lifetime Value)

Trip frequency & seat occupancy rate

Provide predictable KPIs that make financials investor-ready.

Route & Expansion Strategy

Evaluate proposed routes (domestic & cross-border).

Suggest domestic-first route strategy with scalable expansion plan.

Audit UX flows for cultural relevance.

Highlight unique features:

Prayer Stops (automatic mosque suggestions)

Women-Only Rides

Ramadan Mode (iftar/suhoor trips, fasting etiquette)

Suggest ways to embed these features seamlessly into the app experience.

Operational & Technical Improvements

Check for user friction points in onboarding, booking, payments, and package sending.

Identify performance or architecture bottlenecks.

Recommend improvements for React Query, Supabase, and Layered Architecture to ensure scalability.

Investor Appeal Summary

Generate a short, clear summary emphasizing:

Predictable economics

Market differentiation

UX innovations

Growth potential

Output:

Clear actionable recommendations prioritized by impact.

Optimized app architecture diagram with single unified brand.

Suggested metrics dashboard for investor reporting.
