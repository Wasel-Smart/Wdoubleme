Wallet Functional Flows
Ride Payment Flow

1 User books ride
2 Wallet reserves amount
3 Amount moves to escrow
4 Trip completes
5 Escrow releases payment to driver wallet
6 Wasel commission deducted automatically

Package Delivery Flow

Same escrow model as ride payment.

Driver Payout Flow

Driver completes trips
Earnings accumulate in wallet
Driver can withdraw anytime.
