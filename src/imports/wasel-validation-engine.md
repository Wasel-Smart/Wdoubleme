🚀 Wasel Master Validation Engine Prompt

Prompt:

Objective: Validate the Wasel app, a combined ridesharing and package delivery platform for the Middle East. Determine if it deserves investment and effort, its potential benefits, and identify operational, UX, and market gaps. Provide actionable insights, risk-reward evaluation, and a full recommendations report.

Step 1 – Database & Mock Data Setup:

Create these tables:

users (riders & drivers)

vehicles (driver vehicles)

trips (rides & package trips)

packages (package delivery details)

payments (transaction tracking)

Populate tables with smart mock data simulating realistic rides, packages, users, drivers, cities, pricing, and delivery patterns. Include:

50+ users (mix riders/drivers)

Vehicles for all drivers

100+ trips with a mix of rideshare and package delivery

Packages tied to trips with realistic weights and delivery statuses

Payments linked to trips, with realistic success/failure

Step 2 – Analytics & KPI Extraction:
Run queries to calculate:

Total revenue, average revenue per trip, and total trips completed

Driver utilization, earnings, and top-performing drivers

Most popular routes and city pairs

Package delivery success rate

User retention and repeat usage

Step 3 – Market & Gap Analysis:
Analyze the outputs and answer:

Who are the primary users and their adoption likelihood?

What unique pain points does Wasel solve vs. indirect competitors?

Are there operational bottlenecks (backend, trip scheduling, package delivery)?

Which app features increase retention and engagement, and are any missing?

Are driver utilization and route efficiency optimized?

Are revenue streams and pricing sustainable for profitable growth?

Are there regulatory, safety, or trust gaps that could impact adoption?

Which features or processes pose the highest risk or need improvement?

Step 4 – Risk-Reward Evaluation:
Provide a risk-reward scoring system (e.g., 1–5 scale) for:

Revenue potential

Market adoption

Operational feasibility

Technical scalability

Regulatory and safety compliance

Step 5 – Full Recommendations Report:
Deliver a structured report in this format:

Market Insights: [summary + adoption likelihood]

Competitor Analysis: [table with competitors, strengths, weaknesses]

Revenue & Unit Economics: [total revenue, avg revenue/trip, profit estimate]

Operational/Technical Gaps: [list + priority]

UX/Retention Gaps: [list + suggested features]

Regulatory/Future Risks: [list + mitigation]

Overall Recommendation: Go / Optimize / Pause — with reasoning

⚡ Goal: Enable the founder to decide if Wasel is worth scaling, what improvements are essential, and how to maximize market success.
