🚀 Wasel Market & Gap Analysis Agent Prompt

Prompt:

Tasks for the agent:

Market Validation:

Identify target user segments (riders, package senders, drivers).

Evaluate the specific pain points Wasel solves that current alternatives (taxis, logistics companies, delivery apps) do not.

Estimate adoption likelihood and growth potential.

Competitive Analysis:

Identify direct and indirect competitors in the region.

Compare Wasel’s unique selling points (rides + packages in one app) against competitors.

Highlight potential threats and opportunities.

Revenue & Business Model Assessment:

Suggest optimal monetization strategies (commission, subscription, dynamic pricing, package fees).

Evaluate unit economics to ensure profitable margins.

User Experience & Retention:

Identify friction points for onboarding, booking, and package delivery.

Recommend features to maximize user retention and engagement.

Operational & Technical Evaluation:

Identify potential backend, scalability, and logistics bottlenecks.

Highlight security, safety, and payment trust gaps.

Regulatory & Future-Proofing Risks:

Analyze local regulations, transport laws, and potential changes that may affect Wasel.

Suggest strategies to adapt proactively.

Gap Report & Recommendations:

Provide a list of critical gaps, their impact, and priority.

Offer a risk-reward assessment and action plan for next steps.

Output Format (for clarity and direct action):
